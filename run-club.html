FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to sign up before attending?

No, you can just show up! But signing up helps us keep you informed about any schedule changes.

What should I bring?

Just yourself, appropriate running clothes, and a water bottle. We have secure storage for your belongings.

Do you offer post-run refreshments?

Yes! All runners receive a 15% discount on coffee and food after the run.
